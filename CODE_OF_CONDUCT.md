# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone involved in the CRX Review project and community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful**: Treat all community members with kindness and respect
- **Being inclusive**: Welcome individuals of all backgrounds and identities
- **Being constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and constructive criticism
- **Being transparent**: Communicate clearly and openly
- **Being collaborative**: Work together to solve problems and improve the project
- **Being patient**: Understand that people have different perspectives and learn at different paces

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Personal attacks or name-calling
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other characteristics
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Publishing private information without consent
- Threats or intimidation
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Any other conduct that creates an unsafe or unwelcome environment

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All CRX Review project spaces (GitHub, documentation, discussions)
- Public spaces where you represent CRX Review
- Private communications related to project activities

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Report directly**: Email conduct@example.com with details
2. **Include information**:
   - Description of the incident
   - Who was involved
   - When and where it occurred
   - Any relevant context
   - How it affected you

3. **Confidentiality**: Reports will be handled confidentially
4. **Response time**: We aim to respond within 7 days

### Investigation Process

- The project maintainers will investigate all reports
- All parties will be treated fairly
- Confidentiality of all involved will be maintained
- We will determine appropriate action

### Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:

- **Informal resolution**: For minor issues, direct conversation may resolve matters
- **Warnings**: Clear statement that behavior is unacceptable
- **Temporary suspension**: Removal from project for specified period
- **Permanent removal**: Ban from project and community if severe or repeated

Serious violations may result in immediate action without graduated response.

## Responsibilities

### Community Leaders

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action for unacceptable behavior
- Protecting the confidentiality of reporters
- Engaging all community members fairly
- Implementing this Code of Conduct consistently

### Community Members

All community members should:

- Follow this Code of Conduct
- Report violations when observed
- Respect the privacy and safety of others
- Assume good intent while addressing concerns
- Help create a welcoming environment
